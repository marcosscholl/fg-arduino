## Flight Gear (FG) ##
A full on flight simulator environment running can send/receive data over a variety of protocols which can be set at startup.
  * Multiplayer - transmits byte packets in multiplayer mode
  * http - interface with a webbrowser
  * telnet - good olde telnet
  * sockets - recive/transmit/bidirection on a socket. Any number of sockets can be setup.
  * serial - communication to USB and serial


## Backgrounder ##
All paramaters of the sim are made available with in a [property tree](http://wiki.flightgear.org/index.php/Property_Tree).
